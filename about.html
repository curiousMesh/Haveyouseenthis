About this site

This is a personal site where I write about things I notice in the Bible — moments, stories, and words that made me stop and look twice.

I'm not a pastor or a theologian. I'm just someone who reads the Bible and sometimes sees something that surprises me, and wants to share it. These are my thoughts, not doctrine.

The name comes from that feeling — have you seen this? — like when you find something unexpectedly interesting and want to show someone else.

I hope something here makes you go back and read a passage with fresh eyes.
